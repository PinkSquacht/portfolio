Animated gradient webcam frame

Clean gradient animation for your webcam frame (or any other place you want to use it).

Go to widget settings and choose size and gradient colors.

Also, you can use a custom gradient definition with more than 2 colors on "Advanced" tab. The simpliest way to get one it's https://cssgradient.io/. Needs only "linear-gradient" part without "background" keyword.

Set up a duration for which colors will complete a full circle.

Use .png file with transparency as mask. Frame will get a shape of not transparent areas.

Feel free to ask me questions on Twitter - https://twitter.com/kagrayz or Discord - Kagrayz#7637.

Note: Thanks to Sam Woodhall and nutty for YouTube videos that inspired me to create this widget. And to Lx for a nice icon.

Welcome to the new editor

Our mighty Overlay Editor just got better! Creating a stunning looking stream is simpler than ever. Breeze through this short tour to find out how (we don't mind if you just look at the gifs)

  • Click on the '+' button in the corner to add a new one
  • All of your favorite widgets are now nicely organized in categories
  • We've added a search bar so you can find what you are looking for real quick

All editing actions are now placed in single sleek menu. You can also emulate different stream events, to test your work and see how viewers will experience the stream.

P.S. Try dragging the menu!
